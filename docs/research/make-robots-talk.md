The Spinoza-project *[Understanding-Language-By-Machines](http://www.understandinglanguagebymachines.org/)* has funded a follow-up project "Make Robots talk and think" (2020-2024) with 2 PhD students that work on our robot project [*Leolan*i]({{ site.baseurl }}/).
Leolani uses communication to learn about us and the world but she also needs to learn our language at the same time. Communicating and reasoning over the physical world and the people she encounters is a real challenge.
[![]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/uploads/2018/12/27164618_1594080157324160_5808647139012932437_o-300x200.jpg)]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/uploads/2018/12/27164618_1594080157324160_5808647139012932437_o.jpg)They will define their research topic within the [Leolani]({{ site.baseurl }}/) framework and become part of the team of researchers that further develops the platform.
