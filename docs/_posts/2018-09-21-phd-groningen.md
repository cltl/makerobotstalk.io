Me and Piek are invited keynote speakers at the 7th edition of the [Groningen PhD Day](https://phd-day.nl/program/lectures-and-workshops/), Friday September 21st 2018 in the Oosterpoort in Groningen.

The PhD Day is an annual event organized by PhD students of the University of Groningen for PhD and Research Master students to broaden their research and career perspectives.  The PhD Day Groningen is one of the biggest career and educational event for PhDs in Europe. Based on the success of previous editions, we will beexpecting more than 800 PhD students this year. The theme of PhD Day 2018 is: “Imagine, Invent, Inspire”.
