**Talking with Robots**: Children between the ages of 8 and 14 years old introduced themselves to Pepper robot [Leolani](https://www.facebook.com/Leolani.CLTL.VrijeUniversiteitAmsterdam/), asked questions, presented objects and practiced their English, by [@PiekVossen](https://twitter.com/piekvossen) at [VU workshop 06 October 2018](https://www.vu.nl/nl/nieuws-agenda/events/weekend-wetenschap/index.aspx) during [Weekend of Science.](https://www.weekendvandewetenschap.nl/)
